Introducing: Honolulu Answers

Have you ever tried looking for information on a government website? Got lost on Honolulu's city website?

Code for America has partnered with the City and County of Honolulu to launch a new website that's simple, clean, and citizen-focused. Our goal is to make it as easy and accessible as asking a real person.

What We're Doing

You don't need to be a writer to help write quick answers for your city's information and services.

Be part of the first ever civic write-a-thon. Join us in writing compelling, fun answers to YOUR common questions, such as:

  • Where do I get a camping permit?
  • When is my garbage pick-up?
  • How do I renew my driver's license?

Food and writing templates included!
